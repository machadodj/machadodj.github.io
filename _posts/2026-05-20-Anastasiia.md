# Anastasiia Accepts the Lawrence E. Mays Fellowship in Bioinformatics for the 2026–2027 Academic Year

We are thrilled to celebrate Anastasiia for officially accepting the prestigious Lawrence E. Mays Fellowship in Bioinformatics for the upcoming 2026–2027 academic year! 

The Lawrence E. Mays Fellowship is awarded to exceptional Master's and Doctoral students within the Department of Bioinformatics and Genomics at UNC Charlotte. Made possible through the generous financial gifts of donors like Joan Lorden, this fellowship recognizes academic excellence and supports dedicated researchers as they push the boundaries of computational biology and bioinformatics.

Anastasiia’s dedication to her research and academic pursuits embodies the spirit of innovation we strive for in our community. Funding from this fellowship will directly support her work across the Fall 2026 and Spring 2027 semesters, allowing her to focus deeply on advancing her graduate studies and contributing valuable insights to the field.

Please join us in congratulating Anastasiia on this well-deserved honor and wishing her the absolute best in her research endeavors over the coming academic year!
