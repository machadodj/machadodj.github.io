# Reyhaneh Nouri Selected for the William Ribarsky Memorial Scholarship

We are incredibly proud to announce that [Reyhaneh Nouri](https://phyloinformatics.com/members/Reyhaneh_Nouri.html) has been selected as the recipient of the prestigious William Ribarsky Memorial Scholarship for the upcoming academic year! 

Awarded by the Office of the Associate Dean in the College of Computing and Informatics (CCI) at UNC Charlotte, this highly competitive scholarship recognizes students who demonstrate exceptional academic merit and dedication. In a year filled with an impressive pool of applications, the selection committee recognized Reyhaneh’s application as the strongest and most deserving of this honor.

The William Ribarsky Memorial Scholarship stands as a tribute to academic excellence and innovation within the computing community. This award will support Reyhaneh as she continues her academic journey and research pursuits within the college.

Please join us in celebrating Reyhaneh on this outstanding achievement and wishing her continued success in the coming academic year!
